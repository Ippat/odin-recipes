Adobo Chicken


Filipino Chicken Adobo is the national dish of the Philippines and may well become your new favourite Asian chicken dish! Just a few everyday ingredients I can practically guarantee you already have, itâ€™s an effortless recipe that yields juicy, tender chicken coated in a sweet savoury glaze with little pops of heat from peppercorns.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thigh, Skin On
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Bay Leaves
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Combine Water, Garlic, Bay Leaves, Soy Sauce and Vinegar
  2. Marinade Chicken Thighs Overnight
  3. Sear Chicken until brown
  4. Remove Chicken and saute chopped onions
  5. Delgaze with marinade, add chicken, sugar and pepper
  6. Thicken Sauce
