Chicken Stroganoff


Chicken Stroganoff is everything you know and love about Beef Stroganoff… except it’s made with chicken! Golden seared chicken thighs or breast smothered in a sour cream Stroganoff Sauce, this is a quick dinner you can get on the table in 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Yoghurt
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Beef Broth
  • Mushrooms
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Garlic Powder

Instructions

  1. Season Chicken with garlic powder and salt
  2. Brown Chicken, and remove from pan
  3. Saute Mushrooms, then remove
  4. Make roux using butter and flour
  5. Deglaze using beef broth, add mustard, and mushrooms. Reduce
  6. Add Chicken and Yoghurt
  7. Serve with rice or pasta
